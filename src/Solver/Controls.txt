Mouse Controls:
    While holding left click:
        Moving mouse left and right rotates the cube on Y axis (left and right)
        Moving mouse up and down rotates the cube on X axis (up and down)

    While holding right click:
        Moving mouse left and right rotates the cube on Z axis (tilts left and right)

    Scroll wheel:
        Scroll forward - Zooms in
        Scroll back - Zooms out

Keyboard Controls:
    Utility:
        Pressing 1 - Does 100 random moves on the cube
        Pressing 2 - Solves the cube

    Camera:
        W - Moves camera forward
        S - Moves camera backward
        A - Moves camera left
        D - Moves camera right
        Spacebar - Moves camera up
        Alt - Moves camera down

    Cube:
        Ctrl + W: Rotates the TOP face of the cube clockwise (U move)
        Ctrl + S: Rotates the BOTTOM face of the cube clockwise (D move)
        Ctrl + A: Rotates the LEFT face of the cube clockwise (L move)
        Ctrl + D: Rotates the RIGHT face of the cube clockwise (R move)
        Ctrl + Spacebar: Rotates the FRONT face of the cube clockwise (F move)
        Ctrl + C: Rotates the BACK face of the cube clockwise (B move)
        ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        Ctrl + Shift + W: Rotates the TOP face of the cube counter-clockwise (U' move)
        Ctrl + Shift + S: Rotates the BOTTOM face of the cube counter-clockwise (D' move)
        Ctrl + Shift + A: Rotates the LEFT face of the cube counter-clockwise (L' move)
        Ctrl + Shift + D: Rotates the RIGHT face of the cube counter-clockwise (R' move)
        Ctrl + Shift + Spacebar: Rotates the FRONT face of the cube counter-clockwise (F' move)
        Ctrl + Shift + C: Rotates the BACK face of the cube counter-clockwise (B' move)
